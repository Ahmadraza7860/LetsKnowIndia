Palolem Beach




It's known for its beautiful crescent – shape which makes it possible to see both the ends from the centre of the beach. Featured in Matt Damon English Movie The Bourne Supremacy; it's another kind of beach with Green Island; its definitely one of the places to be in.

Agonda Beach




What is special about Agonda Beach? Image result for Agonda Beach Agonda beach is famous for its shining white sands, azure blue water and welcoming shacks. This picturesque scene attracts a lot of tourists around the world. It is also one of the only four beaches in Goa with nesting sites for Olive ridley sea turtles.

Bagga beach




Why is Baga Beach so famous?
Image result for Baga beach Baga Beach is quite famous for its commercial nightlife. The famous Tito's Lane has many bars, lounges, and clubs like Club Tito, Cafe Mambo, Capetown Cafe, Cocktails and Dreams, and Cavala that contribute to keeping the nightlife in Baga Beach, one of the best in Goa..
Is Baga Beach free?
Visitors do not have to pay any entry fee for Baga Beach. Being a public beach, there is no price for Baga Beach entrance. Baga Beach nightlife is famous for beach parties and a night market ideal for shopping.

Cavelossim Beach




Why is Cavelossim beach famous for?
Image result for Cavelossim Beach What is Cavelossim beach famous for? The beach is extremely famous for its location and the adventure watersports offered here in summers and winters. Several tourists come here to enjoy the wide array of water sports such as jet skiing, kayaking, parasailing, Dolphin spotting and a lot more.
Is Cavelossim Beach crowded?
No Crowds, Nice & Clean, No Shacks as this is the time when we usually visit goa when there are no crowds and we enjoy our family time in this prisitne beach a lot.

Benaulim Beach




Why is Benaulim beach famous? Contemporary Benaulim is a popular seaside resort, renowned for its beautiful rice fields, balmy weather and golden sand beaches. It also houses Goa's only Don Bosco Animation Centre.
Can we swim in Benaulim beach?
It is a safe beach for swimming. Nevertheless, don't restrict yourself to Benaulim Beach alone. There are few other swim-friendly beaches in Goa, such as Cavelossim Beach, Palolem Beach, Agonda Beach, Morjim Beach, Mardem Beach, Candolim Beach, and Ashwem Beach.

Candolim Beach




Why is Candolim Beach famous? Nonetheless the Candolim Beach is also famous for hosting India's biggest electronic music dance festival, the Sunburn Festival, which is organized by Percept, an Entertainment, Media and Communications conglomerate.
Candolim is a census town in North Goa and is located in the Bardez taluka in the state of Goa, India. It is situated just south of Calangute Beach and North of Sinquerim.

Varca Beach




Varca beach is about 2 kms south of Benaulim Beach in South Goa. The major attractions around here is the Benaulim Beach, Margao etc. The Varca Beach is one tourist destination that is not so famous in the Beach Capital of India Goa. The quiet resort is charming with the bountiful beauty of nature.

Calangute beach




The Calangute Beach is one of the flagship beaches of Goa and is also famous for the various water sports and activities it hosts. Attracting tourists from all parts, the water sports and activities offered at the Calangute Beach include parasailing, water surfing, banana ride and jet-skiing

Arambol Beach




Why is Arambol Beach famous?
Image result for Arambol Beach
Arambol Beach is also famous for the sweet water lagoon or lake that is located at the end of the adjacent beach i.e. Kalacha beach. It is accessible only on foot and its shallow waters are wonderfully calm to swim in. The sand here is also known to have healing properties.

Morjim Beach




Why is Morjim beach famous? Located in Pernem district, Morjim is renowned as the nesting place for Olive Ridley turtles. During 1997-98, only 5 nests were located and protected on Morjim beach. The number increased to eight in 1998-99 and resulted in extension of the protected area on the beach.

Mandrem Beach




Why is Mandrem Beach famous?
Image result for Mandrem Beach Beautifully tucked between Ashwem and Arambol beaches in North Goa, Mandrem Beach is a serene beach that offers calm and solitude altogether. The beach is covered with magnificent white sands and is lined with palm trees all around its shoreline—no wonder why it is one of the most popular beaches in Goa.
